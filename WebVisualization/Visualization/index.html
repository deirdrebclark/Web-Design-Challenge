Summary: Student Performance vs. Budgets

Visualizations

The purpose of this project was to analyze a city's district-wide standardized test results to aid the school board and mayor in making strategic decisions regarding future school budgets and priorities. The data covers both District and Charter schools in the city.

We will use Matplotlib to compare the city's fifteen high schools' budgets to their students' standardized test scores in math and reading. This site provides visualizations along with explanations of the trends and correlations.
